About the Colloquium

Why PLUMEE 2026

PLUMEE 2026 brings together leading researchers, engineers, and industry professionals from around the world to explore the latest advances in materials, environment, and electronics. This 7th Multidisciplinary Colloquium serves as a platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation across these interconnected fields.

Venue & Location

Where PLUMEE 2026 Takes Place

Mohammed VI Polytechnic University

Conference Facilities

  • Modern conference halls with state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment
  • Spacious exhibition areas for poster presentations
  • Networking lounges and refreshment areas
  • High-speed WiFi throughout the campus
  • Ample parking facilities

Getting There

  • By Air: 45 minutes from Marrakech-Menara Airport
  • By Train: High-speed rail connection to Benguerir
  • By Car: Easy access from Casablanca (1 hour) and Marrakech (45 minutes)
  • Shuttle service available from major hotels

Accommodation

  • UM6P Guest House: On-campus accommodation (walking distance)
  • Partner Hotels: Special rates in Benguerir (10-15 minutes)
  • Marrakech Hotels: Wide range of options (45 minutes from campus)
